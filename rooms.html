BANFF CENTRE - HUT 9

Over three weeks, I developed two independent sound setups inside Hut 9 at the Banff Centre for Arts and Creativity. Each station captured, processed, and sent sound back into the space. They also cross-fed, creating shifting landscapes between balance and chaos. I used grand piano, drums, synths, and movement to activate the systems, then sat in the room and listened until it felt right to respond.

This process generated hours of recordings. This page offers a glimpse into that experience.
